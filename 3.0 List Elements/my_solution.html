Cooking Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour, plus more as needed

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in th flour and mix until combined onto a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
